Delete Your Account – SHARQ BANK

Introduction

Welcome to the account deletion page for SHARQ BANK. To ensure the security and integrity of our banking services, account deletion requests are handled directly by our bank managers. Please follow the instructions below to request the deletion of your account and associated data.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Contact Us: Send an email to our support team at support@sharqbank.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."
  2. Provide Information: Include the following information in your email to verify your identity:
    • Your full name
    • The email address associated with your account
    • Your account username or ID (if applicable)
    • Reason for account deletion request
  3. Verification: Our support team will forward your request to the appropriate bank manager who will verify your request.
  4. Confirmation: Once your request is verified, the bank manager will process the account deletion and you will receive a confirmation email. This process may take some business days.

Data Deletion and Retention Policy

When your account deletion request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email address)
  • Account credentials
  • Transaction history
  • Usage data

Data Retention

Certain data may be retained for legal or operational purposes, including:

  • Transaction records for financial compliance
  • Data required to fulfill any ongoing obligations or legal requirements.
